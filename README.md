## Research Process Summary

### Stage 1: Company Discovery

Companies were sourced from:

- MIDC Industrial Estate Directories
- DSIR Recognized R&D Units
- BSE SME & NSE Emerge Listings
- Industry Expo Directories
- Google Search
- LinkedIn
- Public Industry Databases

### Stage 2: Eligibility Screening

Each company was evaluated against:

#### E1: Producer

Must manufacture products or deliver specialized proprietary services.

#### E2: Accessible

Must have operational presence in India and meaningful operations in Pune.

### Stage 3: Federer Scoring

Companies were scored on:

| Criterion | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| C3 | Differentiation |
| C4 | Decision-Maker Quality |
| C5 | Growing Sector |
| C6 | Growth Signals |
| C7 | Systems Maturity |
| C8 | Leadership Succession |

---

## Key Learnings

- Pune has a strong ecosystem of specialty manufacturing companies.
- Systems maturity is often the strongest predictor of organizational readiness.
- Many specialty manufacturers demonstrate technical differentiation but vary significantly in operational maturity.
- China+1 tailwinds continue to create growth opportunities across specialty chemicals and advanced manufacturing segments.
- Leadership depth and succession planning are important indicators of long-term organizational capability.
